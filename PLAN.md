# AI Village

### What

An AI village containing 10 citizens which act as testbed for public policy measures (from the townhall).

### Why

It's very hard to simulate how public policies will affect real citizens. Real world is hard to simulate - here, we enable agents to act as humans, having access to resources and able to exchange value.

### How

We use 0G compute (https://github.com/0gfoundation/0g-agent-skills) for the agent layer, and [axl](https://github.com/gensyn-ai/axl) as the communication layer.
